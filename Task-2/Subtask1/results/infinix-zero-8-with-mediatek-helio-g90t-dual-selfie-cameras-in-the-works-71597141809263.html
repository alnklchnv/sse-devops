Infinix Zero 8 with MediaTek Helio G90T, dual selfie cameras in the works

  • By
    HT Tech
  • Updated on Aug 11 2020, 04:07 PM IST

Infinix Zero 8 also packs 8GB of RAM, has a 64-megapixel quad camera setup and 33W fast charging speed. 

Infinix is expected to launch the Zero 8 smartphone soon as it has been spotted on the Google Play Console listing site. There’s a teaser video as well showing the phone’s design that has been leaked. Infinix Zero 8 has dual selfie cameras according to the teaser video.

In terms of specifications, Infinix Zero 8 is listed with 8GB of RAM and MediaTek’s Helio G90 chipset. The smartphone runs Android 10, and has a Full HD+ display. The screen size isn’t listed here though. This is based on the Google Play Listing of the Infinix Zero 8 which was first spotted by MSP. The teaser video reveals more specs of the smartphone which will launch along with the Infinix Zero 8i.

Infinix Zero 8/8i Leaked Teaser Video Shows Design and Specifications#InfinixZero8 #InfinixZero8i #Infinix pic.twitter.com/vhZTGIIDPi

— Venkatesh Babu.G (@smartvenkat95) August 7, 2020

Based on the teaser video, the Infinix Zero 8 has a 48-megapixel primary selfie camera and an 8-megapixel wide-angle lens. The smartphone also has a 90Hz display, and 180Hz touch sampling rate. At the rear, the Infinix Zero 8 features a quad camera setup with a 64-megapixel primary sensor with Sony IMX686 sensor. The smartphone also supports slow motion video recording at 960 fps, and has night mode for low-light photography.

More details revealed in the video show the Infinix Zero 8 runs on MediaTek’s Helio G90T processor with 8GB of RAM. This is similar to the Google Play Listing except that it mentioned Helio G90. The smartphone also features a USB Type-C port with support for 33W fast charging speed. It also comes with a liquid cooling system. The video is a proper promotion video so the details are most likely true. There’s no word on when Infinix will launch this phone. It’s also working on the Infinix Hot 10 smartphone.
